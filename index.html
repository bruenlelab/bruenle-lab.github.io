The Lab at UC San Francisco develops high-throughput molecular techniques to study gene regulation. We are funded through the UCSF Sandler Fellows program, and are located in Genentech Hall on UCSF's Mission Bay campus.

We are always looking for motivated graduate students and postdocs! UCSF graduate students and prospective postdocs should contact me to talk about potential projects.
