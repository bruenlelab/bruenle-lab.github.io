The Corces Lab is part of the Gladstone Institute for Neurological Disease and the UCSF Department of Neurology. We combine integrative epigenomic profiling of primary human disease tissue with functional perturbations using in vitro and murine models of Alzheimer's and Parkinson's Disease. The lab will officially launch on July 1st, 2020, and will be located in the Gladstone Institute on the UCSF Mission Bay campus.

We are actively recruiting multiple research technicians to start in July and are always looking for motivated graduate students and postdoctoral fellows to join the team! Interested trainees should contact Ryan to talk about potential projects.
